About Vastra

Vastra is India’s premier online destination for authentic traditional and contemporary Indian apparel. Established in 2010, our mission is to bring the finest craftsmanship and rich heritage to your wardrobe. We work directly with artisans across India to deliver handcrafted sarees, kurtas, lehengas, and accessories that celebrate India’s vibrant culture with a modern touch.
